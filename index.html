What is a Personality Disorder?

There are 10 different personality disorders you could have. They are paranoid, schizoid, schizotypal, antisocial, histrionic, narcissitic, bordeline, avoidant, obsessive-compulsive, and dependent. Only 9% of adults in the US have personality disorders. Borderline and atisocial is the most common personality disorders people have had.
